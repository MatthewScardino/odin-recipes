Pasta Recipe

Description

Delicious Pasta like your grandmotha used to make!

Ingredients:

  • Semolina Flour
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Measure out your Flour
  2. Mix eggs with flour, until dough forms
  3. Let dough rest 24 hours, then roll paper thin
  4. Use pasta cutter to turn large sheets into strips
  5. Boil in water & Eat
